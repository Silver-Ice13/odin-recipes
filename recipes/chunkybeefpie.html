Chunky Beef Pie

Photo by Lee Milo on Unsplash

Description

This is a very basic recipe on creating a chunky beef pie. The ingredients that you add to the slow cooker will be for any gravy, sauce or flavor that you like. Some examples: beef stock, bolognese sauce or curry paste. The main objective is to make the sauce thick rather than a liquid. You could also change type of protein you are using.

Ingredient

  • 500g of beef gravy cut (or any cut of meat).
  • Sauce of your choosing.
  • Pre made short bread pastry sheets.
  • Pre made puff pastry sheets.
  • Salt, pepper and herbs to ones liking.
  • Cooking oil (I prefer sunflower oil, but any non stick oil should do).
  • Optional: corn flour for thickening as needed.
  • Optional: 1 cup of veggies (I like to add a cup of pre soaked mixed beans).

Steps

Cooking the meat filling
  1. Dice the beef into 3cm chunks.
  2. Add oil salt, pepper and herbs to slow cooker. And bring to heat.
  3. Add the meat to sear, stirring gently.
  4. Add the sauce to the meat, then slow cook for 4 hours (or as per your cookers instruction).
  5. Add the cornflour if needed and cook on a low heat until sauce has thickened.

    6. Once cooked and the sauce is thick, add veggies stir though and set to the side.

Cooking the pie
  1. Pre-heat oven to 180c.
  2. Grease a medium round baking dish.
  3. Cover the base of the dish with the short bread pastry, leaving extra over the side.
  4. Add the meat filling to the dish, carefully flattening to the sides.
  5. Cover the top with the puff pastry. Then working around the side of the dish, pinch and twist the two pastries together to seal the pie.
  6. poke two wholes in the centre of the puff pastry.

    7. optional: if you have left over bits of pastry, you can make decoration like leaves and place them on top of the pie

  7. Place in the overn and cook for 1 hour, or until pastry is golden brown

    8. Once cooked, set to the side to cool a little before serving
