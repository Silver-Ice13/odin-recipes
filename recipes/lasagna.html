Description

A simple lasagna dish

Ingredient

  • 500g of beef mince
  • 500g of tomato diced (as close to 500g)
  • pre made pasta sheets (dry or fresh to your liking)
  • 2 teaspoon of tomato paste
  • 1 table spoon of sugar
  • salt and pepper to ones liking
  • ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese (or a hard cheese of choosing to melt on top)
  • cooking oil (I prefer sunflower oil, but any non stick oil should do)
  • optional: italian herb mix

Steps

Cooking the mince and sauces
  1. Pre-heat oil in a large pot on medium high, use enough oil to cover the base
  2. Add mince, salt, pepper and italian herbs. Breakdown the mince by stirring into small bits
  3. Once mince is slightly seared, add diced tomato's, tomato paste and sugar. Cook until tomato's are broken down to a sauces, adjusting heat and stirring to prevent burning.

    4. Once cooked down to a sauces. Turn off stove and set to the side

Layering and cooking lasagna
  1. Pre-heat oven to 180c
  2. Grease a medium baking dish
  3. Add a layer of the sauce to cover the bottom of dish
  4. Add a layer of the pasta sheets to cover the sauce
  5. Add a layer of ricotta cheese
  6. Repeat until sauce is used up, having the top layer be a combination of mince and cheese
  7. Add the grated hard cheese to the top
  8. Place tray in the oven and cook for 50mins or until top layer cheese is golden brown
  9. Slice, serve and enjoy. Can be served with a side of salad and garlic bread
